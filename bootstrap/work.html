The Work

K L Rice Consulting provides facilitation and training to help individuals and organizations to:

  • Assess their organizational climate for diversity, equity and inclusion.

  • Develop, implement and monitor strategic plans to advance values of diversity, equity and inclusion.

  • Build knowledge, skills, awareness and capacity within organizations and/or with organizational partners to advance values of diversity, equity and inclusion.

  • Build community and trust, and engage in effective collaboration within and between organizations.

  • Foster the development of multicultural leaders and community builders.

  • Guide White people through exploring how Whiteness and White social conditioning impact their perceptions, beliefs, attitudes, behavior, decision making, leadership and relationships.

  • Build capacity of White people to dismantle white supremacy and work toward racial justice.

    • K L Rice Consulting supports individuals through one-on-one coaching who want to:

  • Explore how Whiteness and White social conditioning impact their perceptions, beliefs, attitudes, behavior, decision making, leadership and relationships.

  • Build their capacity to dismantle white supremacy.

  • Strengthen their understanding and skills to contribute to effective, sustainable change.
